# India-Specific Automotive Knowledge

## CNG/LPG Systems (Very Common in India)

### CNG Kit Issues

**Common Problems:**
- Kit not switching from petrol to CNG
- Low pressure warning
- Cylinder empty before expected
- Regulator failure
- Injector misfiring on CNG mode

**Symptoms (Hindi):**
- CNG par switch nahi ho raha = Not switching to CNG
- CNG ki pressure kam hai = Low CNG pressure
- CNG cylinder jaldi khatam ho jata hai = CNG runs out quickly
- Petrol pe chalana pad raha hai = Have to run on petrol

**Diagnosis:**
1. Check CNG filter (replace every 10,000 km)
2. Check regulator for ice formation (common in winters)
3. Inspect injectors for carbon buildup
4. Verify cylinder valve operation

### LPG Kit Issues

**Common Problems:**
- Vaporizer failure in cold weather
- Solenoid valve stuck
- Mixer adjustment needed
- Tank leak

**Hindi Terms:**
- LPG se petrol pe aata hai = Switches from LPG to petrol
- Gas ki smell aa rahi hai = Getting gas smell

## Two-Wheeler Issues (India's Largest Segment)

### Starting Problems

**Self Start Issues:**
- Starter motor weak = Self start nahi ho raha
- Battery low = Battery down hai
- Starter switch faulty = Button dabane se start nahi hota

**Kick Start Issues:**
- Kick free ho gaya = Kick not engaging
- Kick jam = Kick stuck
- Decompression issue = Kick bahut hard hai

### Chain/Sprocket

**Symptoms:**
- Chain noise at speed = Chain se awaaz aa rahi hai
- Chain slipping = Chain slip ho rahi hai
- Sprocket teeth worn = Sprocket ghis gaya hai

**Maintenance:**
- Lubricate every 500 km
- Adjust slack: 20-25mm play
- Replace chain and sprocket together

### Clutch Problems

**Common Issues:**
- Hard clutch = Clutch dabane mein zor lagta hai
- Clutch slipping = Gear mein pick-up nahi
- Free play too much = Clutch half-press pe gear lagta hai

### Carburetor/FI Issues

**Carburetor Problems:**
- Starting issue when cold = Subah start nahi hoti
- Idling rough = Idle mein kaanpti hai
- Fuel overflow = Petrol overflow ho raha hai
- Mileage drop = Average gir gayi

**Fuel Injection:**
- FI warning light on
- Injector clogged
- Throttle body dirty

## Indian Road Conditions

### Pothole Damage

**Common Issues:**
- Suspension damage from potholes
- Wheel alignment disturbed
- Tyre sidewall damage
- Rim bent

**Hindi:** Speed breaker/pothole se awaaz aane lagi

### Dust/Monsoon Issues

**Dust:**
- Air filter clogged = Filter jam ho gaya
- AC filter blocked = AC se hawa nahi aa rahi
- Engine performance drop during dusty season

**Monsoon:**
- Water in engine = Paani ghus gaya
- Electrical short in rain
- Brake fade when wet
- Rust on undercarriage
