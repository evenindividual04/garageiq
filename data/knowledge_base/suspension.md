# Suspension & Steering Diagnostic Guide

## Suspension Issues

### Bouncy Ride
- **Cause**: Worn shocks or struts
- **Test**: Bounce test - push down on each corner, should settle within 1-2 bounces
- **Solution**: Replace shocks/struts

### Clunking Noise Over Bumps
- **Cause**: Worn ball joints, control arm bushings, sway bar links
- **Diagnosis**: Visual inspection, check for play
- **Solution**: Replace worn components

### Uneven Tire Wear
- **Inside edge**: Negative camber issue
- **Outside edge**: Positive camber or toe issue
- **Center**: Over-inflation
- **Edges both sides**: Under-inflation

### Vehicle Pulls to One Side
- **Cause**: Alignment, uneven tire pressure, brake drag
- **First check**: Tire pressures

## Steering Problems

### Power Steering Whine
- **Cause**: Low fluid, air in system, pump failure
- **Check**: Fluid level and condition
- **Solution**: Top up, bleed, or replace pump

### Stiff Steering
- **Cause**: Low PS fluid, pump failure, rack issue
- **Diagnosis**: Check fluid, check belt

### Vibration in Steering Wheel
- **At low speed**: Tire balance, wheel bearing
- **At highway speed**: Wheel balance, alignment
- **Only when braking**: Warped rotors

### Play in Steering Wheel
- **Cause**: Worn tie rod ends, steering gear
- **Diagnosis**: Check for play in components

## Hindi Terms
- Steering bhaari hai = stiff steering
- Steering mein vibration = steering vibration  
- Gaadi ek taraf jaati hai = car pulls to one side
- Suspension mein awaaz = suspension noise
- Dhakke lag rahe hain = bumpy ride
