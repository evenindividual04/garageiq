# Electrical System Diagnostic Guide

## Battery Issues

### Dead Battery / Won't Start
- **Voltage Test**: Should be 12.6V resting, 14.2V running
- **Load Test**: Battery should hold under load
- **Age**: Most batteries last 3-5 years
- **Terminals**: Corrosion prevents power flow

### Symptoms
- Slow crank
- Clicking sound
- Dim lights
- Accessories not working

## Starter Problems

### Clicking Sound Only
- **Battery**: May be too weak to turn starter
- **Starter Solenoid**: Click = solenoid working but starter not
- **Connections**: Check starter bolts, ground

### Grinding Noise
- **Flywheel**: Damaged teeth
- **Starter Gear**: Worn bendix

## Alternator Issues

### Battery Light On
- **Belt**: Check tension and condition
- **Alternator Output**: Should be 13.8-14.5V
- **Connections**: Check wiring

### Signs of Failure
- Dim lights
- Burning smell
- Whining noise
- Multiple electrical issues

## Hindi Terms
- Gadi start nahi ho rahi = car won't start
- Battery dead = battery khatam
- Light dim ho rahi = lights dimming
