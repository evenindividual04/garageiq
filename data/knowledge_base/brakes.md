# Brake System Diagnostic Guide

## Common Brake Noises

### Squealing
- **Cause**: Brake pads worn to wear indicator
- **Diagnosis**: Visual inspection of pad thickness, should be >3mm
- **Solution**: Replace pads, check rotors for scoring

### Grinding
- **Cause**: Metal-to-metal contact, pads completely worn
- **Urgency**: HIGH - Stop driving immediately
- **Solution**: Replace pads and rotors

### Pulsating Pedal
- **Cause**: Warped rotors from heat
- **Diagnosis**: Runout test on rotors
- **Solution**: Resurface or replace rotors

## Brake Fluid Issues

### Spongy Pedal
- **Cause**: Air in brake lines, low fluid, moisture contamination
- **Diagnosis**: Check fluid level, test for boiling point
- **Solution**: Bleed brakes, replace fluid if contaminated

### Low Fluid Level
- **Cause**: Leak in system or worn pads
- **Check**: Master cylinder, calipers, lines

## ABS Problems

### ABS Warning Light
- **Cause**: Wheel speed sensor, control module, wiring
- **Diagnosis**: Scan ABS codes
- **Common Codes**: C0035 (LF sensor), C0040 (RF sensor)
