# Engine Diagnostic Guide

## No Start Conditions

### Engine Cranks But Won't Start
- **Check Fuel**: Listen for fuel pump prime, check fuel pressure
- **Check Spark**: Remove plug, ground, crank, look for spark
- **Check Compression**: Compression test, should be 150+ PSI

### Engine Won't Crank
- **Battery**: Test voltage, should be 12.6V resting
- **Starter**: Listen for click, test starter motor
- **Connections**: Check ground straps, battery terminals

## Overheating

### Temperature Gauge High
- **Coolant Level**: Check when cold, inspect for leaks
- **Thermostat**: May be stuck closed
- **Water Pump**: Check for weep hole leak
- **Radiator**: Check for blockage, fan operation

### Symptoms
- Steam from hood
- Sweet smell (coolant)
- Temperature warning light
- Loss of power

## Rough Idle

### Causes
- Vacuum leaks
- Dirty throttle body
- Faulty MAF/MAP sensor
- Bad spark plugs
- Fuel injector issues

### Diagnosis
1. Check for codes (P0300 series = misfire)
2. Listen for vacuum leaks
3. Clean throttle body
4. Inspect spark plugs
