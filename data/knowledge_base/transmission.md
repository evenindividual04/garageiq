# Transmission Diagnostic Guide

## Common Problems

### Hard Shifting
- **Cause**: Low fluid, worn clutch packs, solenoid issues
- **Diagnosis**: Check fluid level and color (should be red, not brown/burnt)
- **Solution**: Fluid change, solenoid replacement

### Slipping
- **Symptoms**: Engine revs but car doesn't accelerate
- **Cause**: Worn clutch packs, low fluid, torque converter
- **Diagnosis**: Stall test, pressure test
- **Solution**: Rebuild or replacement

### Delayed Engagement
- **Symptoms**: Delay when shifting from P to D or R
- **Cause**: Low fluid, internal seals worn
- **Diagnosis**: Check fluid, scan for codes

## Transmission Fluid

### When to Change
- Every 30,000-60,000 miles for most vehicles
- Immediately if fluid is brown, burnt smell

### Fluid Colors
- **Red/Pink**: Good
- **Dark Red**: Change soon
- **Brown/Black**: Damaged, change immediately

## CVT Issues

### Common Problems
- Belt slipping
- Juddering during acceleration
- Overheating

### Hindi Terms
- Gear change nahi ho raha = won't shift
- Gear slip ho raha hai = transmission slipping
- Awaaz aa rahi hai gear mein = noise from transmission
