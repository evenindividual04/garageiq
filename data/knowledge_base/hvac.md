# HVAC Diagnostic Guide

## AC Not Cooling

### No Cold Air
- **Refrigerant**: Low charge is #1 cause
- **Compressor**: Check if clutch engages
- **Condenser Fan**: Must run when AC on
- **Blend Door**: Could be stuck on heat

### Diagnosis Steps
1. Check AC clutch engagement
2. Measure pressures (low/high side)
3. Normal: Low=25-40 PSI, High=200-250 PSI
4. Low both sides = low refrigerant
5. High both sides = condenser issue

## Heater Not Working

### No Heat
- **Coolant Level**: Low coolant = no heat
- **Thermostat**: Stuck open = no heat
- **Heater Core**: Could be clogged
- **Blend Door**: Stuck on cold

### Test
- Feel heater hoses, both should be hot
- If only one hot = clogged heater core

## Blower Issues

### No Airflow
- **Fuse**: Check blower fuse first
- **Resistor**: Common failure, speed issues
- **Motor**: Listen for hum, test with voltmeter
- **Relay**: Check for power at connector

## Hindi Terms
- AC thanda nahi = AC not cold
- Heater kaam nahi = heater not working
- Hawa nahi aa rahi = no airflow
