# Common Automotive Symptoms & Diagnosis

*Reference: Standard automotive diagnostic procedures*

## Engine Symptoms

### Won't Start

**Symptoms:** Key turns, no crank or crank but no start

**Diagnostic Steps:**
1. **No crank, no click:** Battery dead, starter relay, neutral safety switch
2. **Click only:** Weak battery, bad starter solenoid, corroded connections
3. **Crank, no start:** No fuel (pump), no spark (ignition), no compression

**Hindi:** Gadi start nahi ho rahi

---

### Engine Overheating

**Symptoms:** Temperature gauge high, steam from hood, sweet smell

**Causes (most common first):**
1. Low coolant level - check for leaks
2. Thermostat stuck closed
3. Water pump failure
4. Radiator blocked or clogged
5. Cooling fan not working
6. Head gasket failure

**Diagnosis:**
- Check coolant level when COLD
- Scan for temp sensor codes (P0115-P0118)
- Check fan operation
- Pressure test cooling system for leaks

**Hindi:** Gadi garam ho rahi hai, Engine garam

---

### Rough Idle / Misfiring

**Symptoms:** Engine shakes at idle, runs rough, check engine light

**Common Causes:**
1. Worn spark plugs (replace every 30-100k miles)
2. Ignition coil failure
3. Vacuum leak (hissing sound)
4. Dirty fuel injectors
5. Mass Air Flow sensor dirty

**Related DTCs:** P0300-P0306, P0171, P0172

**Hindi:** Engine rough chal rahi hai

---

### Loss of Power

**Symptoms:** Car accelerates slowly, no pick-up

**Causes:**
1. Clogged air filter
2. Clogged fuel filter
3. Worn spark plugs
4. Clogged catalytic converter (P0420)
5. Turbo failure (if equipped)
6. Transmission slipping

**Hindi:** Pick-up nahi mil rahi, Power nahi aa rahi

---

## Brake Symptoms

### Squealing Brakes

**Symptoms:** High-pitched squeal when braking

**Causes:**
1. Brake pad wear indicators - pads need replacement
2. Glazed brake pads - aggressive braking
3. Dust or debris between pad and rotor
4. Cheap brake pads

**Diagnosis:**
- Visual inspection: pads should be >3mm thick
- Check rotors for scoring

**Hindi:** Brake lagane par awaaz

---

### Grinding Brakes

**Symptoms:** Metal-on-metal grinding sound

**Severity:** CRITICAL - Stop driving immediately

**Cause:** Brake pads completely worn, metal backing hitting rotor

**Solution:** Replace pads AND rotors (rotors will be damaged)

**Hindi:** Brake mein ghisne ki awaaz

---

### Spongy Brake Pedal

**Symptoms:** Pedal goes to floor, soft feel

**Causes:**
1. Air in brake lines - bleed brakes
2. Low brake fluid - check for leaks
3. Worn master cylinder seals
4. Brake fluid absorbing moisture

**Hindi:** Brake neeche tak ja raha hai

---

### Brake Pedal Pulsation

**Symptoms:** Pedal vibrates when braking

**Cause:** Warped brake rotors from heat

**Solution:** Resurface or replace rotors

**Hindi:** Brake lagane par vibration

---

## Electrical Symptoms

### Battery Keeps Dying

**Causes:**
1. Old battery (3-5 year lifespan)
2. Alternator not charging
3. Parasitic drain (something staying on)
4. Corroded battery terminals
5. Loose battery connections

**Diagnosis:**
- Test battery voltage: 12.6V when off, 14.2V running
- Check alternator output
- Parasitic draw test

**Hindi:** Battery dead ho jaati hai, Battery charge nahi ho rahi

---

### Dim Lights

**Symptoms:** Headlights dim, especially at idle

**Causes:**
1. Weak alternator
2. Poor ground connection
3. Weak battery
4. High resistance in wiring

**Hindi:** Light dim ho rahi hai

---

## Transmission Symptoms

### Hard Shifting

**Symptoms:** Gear changes are jerky or delayed

**Causes:**
1. Low transmission fluid
2. Worn clutch packs
3. Shift solenoid failure
4. Valve body issue

**Related DTCs:** P0700, P0730-P0734

**Hindi:** Gear change karne mein problem

---

### Transmission Slipping

**Symptoms:** Engine revs but car doesn't accelerate

**Causes:**
1. Low fluid level
2. Worn clutch packs
3. Torque converter failure
4. Internal hydraulic leak

**Severity:** Moderate-High - get checked soon

**Hindi:** Gear slip ho raha hai

---

## Suspension Symptoms

### Bouncy Ride

**Symptoms:** Car keeps bouncing over bumps

**Cause:** Worn shock absorbers or struts

**Test:** Push down on each corner - should settle in 1-2 bounces

**Hindi:** Gadi bahut uchhal rahi hai

---

### Clunking Over Bumps

**Symptoms:** Knocking sounds from suspension

**Causes:**
1. Worn ball joints
2. Worn control arm bushings  
3. Worn sway bar links
4. Loose strut mount

**Hindi:** Speed breaker par awaaz
