# Tire and Wheel Diagnostic Guide

## Tire Issues

### Puncture / Flat
- **Visual Inspection**: Look for nail, screw, debris
- **Soap Test**: Spray soapy water, bubbles show leak
- **Location**: Sidewall = not repairable

### Low Pressure
- **TPMS Light**: Indicates 25%+ low
- **Check All 4**: Temperature affects pressure
- **Slow Leak**: Check valve stem, bead

### Uneven Wear
- **Inside/Outside**: Alignment issue
- **Center Wear**: Over-inflation
- **Edge Wear**: Under-inflation
- **Cupping**: Suspension problem

## Wheel Problems

### Vibration While Driving
- **Balance**: Weights fallen off
- **Bent Rim**: Hit pothole
- **Tire Defect**: Belt separation

### Speed-Related
- 40-50 mph = front balance
- 60+ mph = rear balance or alignment

## Hindi Terms
- Tyre puncture = tire flat
- Hawa kam hai = low pressure
- Steering mein vibration = wheel vibration
